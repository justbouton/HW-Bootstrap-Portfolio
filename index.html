About Me

Welcome to my first responsive BootStrap UCB webpage, it's a work in progress...

Since the age of 13 I've been fascinated with computers. By the age 15 I landed a full time job building custom computers! Most of the computers I build were high-end overclocked systems such as water-cooled and VapoChill© towers "refrigerated tower." More recently, "the past few years" I've been dabbling with bash scripts with some pretty good results. Every-time I create code and see it in action it reminds me of my first Ubuntu Apache2 server. The landing page said; "It Works!" I've been addicted to coding ever since. Special thanks to my mentor and great friend Larry.
